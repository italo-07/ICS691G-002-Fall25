# Report

## Article Information

**Title:** Black hole collision confirms decades-old predictions by Einstein and Hawking

**Source:** CNN

**Link:** [https://www.cnn.com/2025/09/11/science/black-hole-collision-einstein-hawking](https://www.cnn.com/2025/09/11/science/black-hole-collision-einstein-hawking)

## Summary

Astronomers have observed a black hole collision, named GW250114, with high clarity using the LIGO observatories, confirming predictions made by Albert Einstein and Stephen Hawking decades ago. The event involved two black holes, each about 30–35 times the mass of the sun, merging nearly 1 billion light years away into a single, rapidly spinning black hole. Thanks to upgrades in LIGO’s instruments, scientists captured the gravitational waves from the collision in far greater detail than ever before. This allowed them to confirm Roy Kerr’s theory that black holes can be fully described by just mass and spin, as well as Hawking’s surface area theorem, which states that the surface area of black holes can never decrease.
